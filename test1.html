How to Test My template?

Microsoft IIS (Internet Information Services)

1. Locate the "C:\inetpub\wwwroot\domainname" directory
2. Remove everything including this file, and move your Template folders into this directory.

Linux Apache

1. Change your directory to "/usr/share/nginx/html/"

[root@wbsrvr ~]$ cd /usr/share/nginx/html/

2. Move your things!

[root@wbsrvr ~]$ mv -d /path/to/template .
